Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help expand the business of Hatch Photography. We want to make it easier for people to make appointments, and to keep appointments organized. We also want to advertise some of our work here, so that people will be able to see what we can provide. The services provided include portraits, family and group, engagement, bridal and groom, weddings, and receptions. We also want to show affordable pricing, between $100 to $200 per hour.

Audience

We are here to take your picture! If you got engaged or are getting married, we are here for you. We also take family photos. If you have a major event coming up, we can take pictures to help you celebrate. We can help you take photos to help you decorate your home. We also take pictures of your sweet fur babies; we know they are an important part of your family. We are here for all your photography needs!
